# Neon Vaporwave Pong Game

A retro-futuristic Pong game with neon vaporwave aesthetics, featuring dynamic AI that ramps up in difficulty, stunning visual effects, and immersive sound design. Built as a web game using HTML5 Canvas, CSS, and JavaScript.

![Game Screenshot](Screenshot%202026-04-13%20021025.png)

## 🌟 Features

- **Neon Vaporwave Theme**: Vibrant cyan, magenta, and purple gradients with CRT-style scanlines and vignette effects.
- **Adaptive AI Opponent**: The AI difficulty increases as the game progresses, starting easy and becoming progressively harder.
- **Visual Effects**: Ball trails, particle bursts, spark effects, and screen shake for impactful gameplay.
- **Sound Effects**: Retro beeps and tones for hits, scores, and game events (toggleable).
- **Slow-Motion Match Points**: Dramatic slow-mo sequences during crucial moments.
- **Responsive Controls**: Mouse, touch, and keyboard support.
- **Pause and Restart**: Easy game management with keyboard shortcuts.

## 🎮 How to Play

Classic Pong gameplay: Hit the ball back and forth with your paddle. First to 11 points wins!

- **Objective**: Prevent the ball from reaching your side while scoring against the AI.
- **Scoring**: Score points by getting the ball past the AI's paddle.
- **AI Progression**: The AI gets faster, more accurate, and its paddle shrinks as the score increases.

## 🕹️ Controls

- **Mouse/Touch**: Move your paddle up and down.
- **Keyboard**:
  - `W` / `↑` (Arrow Up): Move paddle up
  - `S` / `↓` (Arrow Down): Move paddle down
  - `Space`: Start game / Pause/Resume
  - `R`: Restart game
  - `M`: Toggle sound on/off
- **Buttons**:
  - Sound toggle
  - FX (visual effects) toggle
  - Pause button

## 💡 Tips

- **Angle Shots**: Hit the edges of your paddle for sharper angles.
- **Rallies**: Longer rallies increase ball speed slightly.
- **AI Adaptation**: Stay calm during match points and use paddle edges effectively.
